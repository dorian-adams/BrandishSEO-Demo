Strategy Overview

Every section of your SEO strategy at-a-glance

Keyword Research

Get a comprehensive keyword report with actionable insights to ensure successful implementation.

  • Associated search volume and difficulty metrics
  • Silo structure and internal linking recommendations
  • Recommended content length and sub-keywords for added relevance

Content Strategy

Receive editorial guidelines and a content calendar to structure and optimize your content for maximum organic performance.

  • Procedures to keep content fresh and relevant
  • Heading tag usage and recommended article length
  • Actionable promotion strategies (social media and link building)

Link Building

Gain a tailored list of target websites for backlinks, complete with actionable tactics to secure them.

  • Step-by-step instructions for each tactic
  • Proven methods: content marketing, guest blogging, outreach, and press
  • Custom email templates for outreach campaigns

Competitor Analysis

Discover what drives your top competitor's success and gain a clear plan to surpass them.

  • Comprehensive analysis of your highest-ranking competitor
  • Focused on eliminating competitor advantages for long-term success

Content Audit

Get a detailed evaluation of your existing content with actionable insights to optimize performance.

  • Comprehensive analysis of key content pages
  • Technical checks, including canonicalization and duplicate content issues
  • Evaluation for uniqueness, keyword targeting, length, and overall value

Link Audit

Receive a thorough evaluation of your website's backlink profile.

  • Gain clarity on your backlink health and steps to strengthen it
  • Detect link schemes such as paid links, sponsored content, or link exchanges
  • Recommendations for remediation, including ending schemes or adding "no-follow" attributes

E-A-T

Establish your website as a trusted authority by meeting Google's standards for Expertise, Authority, and Trustworthiness (E-A-T), especially vital for YMYL (Your Money or Your Life) websites.

  • Assess how effectively your website communicates expertise and authority
  • Recommendations to improve credibility and comply with E-A-T best practices

Technical

Uncover and address coding-related factors that may hinder your website's organic performance.

  • Analyze meta titles, meta descriptions, and heading tag implementation
  • Assess site speed, performance, and mobile usability
  • Prioritized recommendations for easy implementation
